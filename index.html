About

Learn more about who we are.

Online shopping can often be a tedious and frustrating routine. Comparing prices and products takes time and usually results in several open tabs in your browser. In an attempt to alleviate the irritation and time-consumption of online shopping, we created a webpage that shows side-by-side comparisons of search results. White Shirt is a website that cross references online stores based on keyword searches, and displays the results of the selected stores cheek by jowl for efficiency, in hopes of making any online shopping experience an enjoyable one.
